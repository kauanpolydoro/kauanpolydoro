I build backend systems for warehouse operations where correctness, traceability, and operational continuity matter.

My background combines database-centric WMS implementations with modern backend architecture. I began my career building and maintaining logistics systems based on **Oracle Database** and **PL/SQL**, with business rules implemented through procedures, packages, functions, triggers, and database-driven workflows.

Today, I also work with **TypeScript**, **Node.js**, **Bun**, **PostgreSQL**, **SQL Server**, **RabbitMQ**, **Docker**, and Domain-Driven Design principles.

- Sorter, conveyor, miniload, and put-to-light automation interfaces
